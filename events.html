AAMI Guest Speaker, Demetrius Wright

GSW's African American Male Initiative (AAMI) will be hosting guest speaker and GSW alumnus, Mr. Demetrius Wright in Wheatley Administration Building, Room 127.

Mr. Wright will present a workshop and speech about how to foster relationships with professors, staff, and like-minded peers.

He will show examples of how to prioritize your responsibilities of academic excellence and social responsibilities and will use his own experience as a student at GSW to provide a guideline for students to follow so that they too will become a GSW graduate.

This is a W2W Event
04:30 PM - 05:30 PM
Wheatley Administration Building, Room 127

NPHC Stroll Off & Cookout

The National Pan-Hellenic Council at Georgia Southwestern State University is hosting our annual stroll-off with a cookout!

Tickets for the stroll-off are $5 in advance and $7 at the door, and participants will be competing for a $250 cash prize and the NPHC spirit stick.

This will take place on April 26, 2023 beginning with the cookout at 5PM on Centennial Plaza followed by the stroll-off in the IM Gym at 7PM. Doors will open for the stroll-off at 6PM. The cookout will be free!
